The Pollinator Garden

The FOGs Pollinator Garden is located in the grass strip of the Middlesex parking lot, east of Middlesex College on Western University’s campus. Launched in 2021 and planted in May 2022, the garden features 1,300 plants across 30 species — most native to the Carolinian Life Zone. It expanded in 2023 after a successful first season. Western holds a Bee City Canada designation.

The garden is coordinated by Blanca Alvarez and maintained by FOGs volunteers, with a focus on promoting biodiversity, seasonal stewardship, and ecological education.
