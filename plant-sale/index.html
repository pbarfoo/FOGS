Plant List

Our list changes each season as plants become available. If you don’t see something you’re after, ask a volunteer or email us — we may be able to help. Plants can also be viewed or collected by appointment.

Common name Botanical name Type Notes
Alpine Aster Aster alpinus Perennial Low-growing, purple flowers in early summer
Creeping Phlox Phlox subulata Perennial Excellent ground cover; pink or white
Hens and Chicks Sempervivum tectorum Succulent Hardy, drought-tolerant; many forms
Creeping Thyme Thymus serpyllum Perennial Fragrant mat; attracts pollinators
Stonecrop Sedum spp. Succulent Various heights and colours
Saxifrage Saxifraga spp. Perennial Ideal for rock crevices
Aubrieta Aubrieta deltoidea Perennial Cascading purple blooms in spring
Dwarf Iris Iris pumila Bulb Compact, very early bloomer
Basket of Gold Aurinia saxatilis Perennial Bright yellow in spring
Rock Cress Arabis caucasica Perennial Fragrant white flowers; spreading
Maiden Pink Dianthus deltoides Perennial Cheerful mat-forming bloomer
Alpine Columbine Aquilegia alpina Perennial Delicate blue flowers; self-seeds gently

Availability changes each season. Contact info@fogsuwo.ca for the current list or to ask about a specific plant.
