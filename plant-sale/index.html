Plant Sale

Annual Plant Sale

Each spring, FOGs holds its popular annual plant sale on the Western University campus. Our volunteers propagate and grow a wide variety of plants, and our knowledgeable team is always on hand to offer advice on plant care and selection.

Proceeds from the plant sale go towards supporting a community program and providing bursaries for students at Western University.

Spring Sale: The week of May 27th, by the greenhouses on the UWO campus.

Summer Sales: Mondays from June 1 – September 28, 12:30 – 1:30 pm.

Location: Between the Jancey Garden and the greenhouses — look for the fenced area just behind the tropical greenhouses.

Plant List

Our plant list is updated regularly. Below is a sample of the types of plants typically available. If you don’t see something you’re looking for, feel free to ask our volunteers or email us at info@fogsuwo.ca.

Common Name Botanical Name Type Notes
Alpine Aster Aster alpinus Perennial Low-growing, purple flowers
Creeping Phlox Phlox subulata Perennial Excellent ground cover for rock gardens
Hens and Chicks Sempervivum tectorum Succulent Hardy, drought-tolerant
Creeping Thyme Thymus serpyllum Perennial / Herb Fragrant, attracts pollinators
Stonecrop Sedum spp. Succulent Various colours available
Saxifrage Saxifraga spp. Perennial Ideal for rock garden crevices
Aubrieta Aubrieta deltoidea Perennial Cascading purple blooms in spring
Dwarf Iris Iris pumila Perennial / Bulb Compact, early bloomer
Basket of Gold Aurinia saxatilis Perennial Bright yellow spring flowers
Rock Cress Arabis caucasica Perennial White flowers, ground cover

Plant availability changes each season. Contact us for the current list.

Why Buy from FOGs?

  • Plants propagated by knowledgeable volunteer gardeners with decades of experience
  • Many varieties well-suited to London, Ontario’s climate
  • Expert advice available from our volunteers at every sale
  • Proceeds support Western University student bursaries and a community program
  • Unique plants not always found at commercial nurseries

Don’t see what you’re looking for? Email info@fogsuwo.ca and our volunteers will do their best to help. Plants can also be viewed or picked up by appointment.
