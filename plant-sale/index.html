The Plant Sale

When & Where
Spring sale Week of May 27th, beside the greenhouses on the UWO campus
Summer sales Every Monday, June 1 – September 28, 12:30 – 1:30 pm
Location Between the Jancey Garden and the tropical greenhouses, Western University, London ON — look for the fenced area just behind the tropical greenhouses
Can’t make it? Email info@fogsuwo.ca and we’ll do our best to accommodate. Plants can be viewed or picked up by appointment.
Proceeds Over $50,000 raised in bursaries for undergraduate science students at Western, plus support for a community program
What We Sell

FOGs usually has for sale: perennials, biennials, native perennials, trees, shrubs, herbs, vegetables, annuals, and houseplants. Every plant is propagated and grown by our volunteers, and our knowledgeable team is on hand at every sale to advise on care and placement.

Our plant list is updated regularly. If you don’t see something you’re looking for, ask a volunteer at the sale or email info@fogsuwo.ca.
