Plant Sale Location

The plant sale is held between the Jancey Garden and the greenhouses at Western University, London, Ontario. Look for the fenced area just behind the tropical greenhouses.

The St. Mary's Cement Rock Garden — where our volunteers work year-round — lies between the Collip Building and the Biological & Geological Sciences Building, stretching down from Middlesex Drive to the lower greenhouses.
