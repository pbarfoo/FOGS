General Inquiries

For questions about plants, volunteering, or anything else related to Friends of the Gardens, please email us at:

info@fogsuwo.ca

You are also welcome to visit us at the plant sale to speak with one of our knowledgeable volunteers in person.

Plant Appointments

If you would like to view or pick up plants outside of regular sale hours, you can arrange an appointment by emailing us at:

info@fogsuwo.ca

If you don’t see a plant you’re looking for on our list, feel free to ask — our volunteers may be able to help.

Plant Sale Location

The plant sale is held between the Jancey Garden and the greenhouses at Western University, London, Ontario, Canada. Look for the fenced area just behind the tropical greenhouses.

Address: Western University, London, Ontario, Canada

Landmark: Fenced area between the Jancey Garden and the tropical greenhouses

Spring Sale: Week of May 27th

Summer Sales: Mondays, June 1 – September 28, 12:30 – 1:30 pm

Find Us on Campus

The St. Mary's Cement Rock Garden, where our volunteers work, is located between the Collip Building and the Biological & Geological Sciences Building, stretching down from Middlesex Drive to the lower greenhouses.

Western University is located at 1151 Richmond Street, London, Ontario, N6A 3K7.
