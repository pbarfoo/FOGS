Welcome to Friends of the Gardens

FOGs is a community of volunteer gardeners dedicated to nurturing and maintaining the beautiful gardens of Western University in London, Ontario, Canada.

About FOGs

Friends of the Gardens (FOGs) is a group of volunteer gardeners at Western University. Volunteers include Western staff, faculty, students, and others from the broader community who share a love of horticulture.

FOGs volunteers work in the St. Mary's Cement Rock Garden, which lies between the Collip Building and the Biological & Geological Sciences Building, stretching down from Middlesex Drive to the lower greenhouses. The group has been quietly and colourfully growing for nearly 30 years.

Plant Sale

Our annual plant sale takes place in late May by the greenhouses on the UWO campus. Plants are also available on select Mondays throughout the summer.

Proceeds from FOGs plant sales go towards supporting a community program and bursaries for students at Western!

Get Involved

Interested in joining FOGs? Whether you are a Western staff member, student, or community member with a passion for gardening, we welcome new volunteers.

Periodic workshops are held on topics such as making troughs, pruning, taking cuttings, grafting, plant care, and plant identification.
