The Rock Garden

About FOGs

Annual Plant Sale

Each spring and through the summer, we sell propagated perennials, natives, shrubs, herbs, vegetables, annuals, and houseplants. Proceeds have raised over $50,000 in student bursaries at Western.

Pollinator Garden

FOGs also tends the Western University Pollinator Garden — a green space supporting bees, butterflies, and other insects vital to a healthy campus ecosystem.

Who We Are

FOGs volunteers include undergraduate students, alumni, staff, and retired staff from across the Western University campus — as well as members of the broader London community. If you share a love of plants and gardening, you are welcome.

Email info@fogsuwo.ca to get involved or ask about the next volunteer day.
