The Rock Garden

Our volunteers maintain the St. Mary's Cement Rock Garden between the Collip Building and the Biological & Geological Sciences Building — a beloved campus landmark for nearly three decades.

Annual Plant Sale

Each spring and through the summer, we sell a wide variety of propagated plants. Proceeds support a community program and student bursaries at Western.

Pollinator Garden

FOGs volunteers also tend the Western University Pollinator Garden, a green space designed to support bees, butterflies, and other beneficial insects on campus.

Join us at the Plant Sale

Spring sale runs the week of May 27th by the UWO greenhouses. Summer sales are every Monday, June 1 – September 28, 12:30–1:30 pm. Look for the fenced area just behind the tropical greenhouses.

Get Involved

Everyone Is Welcome

Whether you are a Western staff member, student, faculty, or community member with a passion for horticulture, FOGs welcomes you. Members take part in regular volunteer days, periodic workshops on propagation and plant care, and the annual plant sale.

To learn more or get in touch, email us at info@fogsuwo.ca.
